Retail Saarthi: AI-Driven Inventory Intelligence

A multilingual AI agent built to help Indian kirana stores optimize inventory through demand prediction and trend analysis.Done by me and my team at CMR University

GitHub Repo

The Problem

Unorganized retailers in India often face inventory overhang due to a lack of data-driven forecasting. Retail Saarthi bridges this gap with a simple, mobile-first solution designed specifically for the Indian market, replacing intuition with precise insights.

The Methodology

I orchestrated the agentic workflows using LangGraph to ensure reliable, multi-step decision-making. To make the tool accessible, I utilized a Qwen 3.5 B SLM and Sarvam M model, enabling the agent to communicate and analyze trends in English, Hindi, Telugu, Kannada, and Tamil.

The Impact

The goal is to eliminate waste and financial loss for small retailers by replacing intuition with precise, AI-generated insights. By providing trend analysis in local languages, we empower shop owners to compete with modern organized retail.
