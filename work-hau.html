Hauwee's Degen Diaries: Dev Log

A series of fully AI-produced episodes designed to make crypto safety easy to understand through storytelling.

System Methodology

I orchestrated a multi-stage production workflow that manually bridged independent generative tools into a cohesive cinematic output. By following a structured path from script verification through Veo 3 motion synthesis (via Google Flow) and Eleven Labs audio, I ensured that every AI asset was hand-vetted and refined before final assembly in DaVinci Resolve.

The Creative Challenge

Maintaining character identity and temporal consistency during intense motion sequences required a hands-on approach. I solved character 'drift' by manually implementing a reference-image anchoring strategy and iteratively refining custom ComfyUI nodes until the visual output met professional standards.

Educational Objective

I leveraged the Hauwee character to translate complex Web3 security protocols into accessible, narrative-driven content. My role was to act as the human bridge—ensuring that the AI-generated story remained technically accurate while pushing the boundaries of what high-fidelity AI video can achieve.

Character Development

I started with a rough idea and manually moved through these stages to lock in a look that stayed consistent throughout the series.

Initial Concept Developing the base personality and look using ChatGPT and high-level prompt tuning.
Prompting Expressions Prompting to add expressions for the character to ensure emotional depth and consistency.
