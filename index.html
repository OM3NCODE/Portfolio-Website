About Me

I operate at the intersection of Design Thinking and Agentic Engineering. While I identify as a 'Vibe Coder' who uses AI to build applications at 10x speed, I apply a Technical-First philosophy. I don't just accept AI outputs; I build the systems—utilizing tools like LangGraph and Chroma DB—to tame them. For the past two years at High As Unicorn (HAU), my role has expanded from an A.I content creator to a Secondary Developer on Standby, helping create the technical infrastructure needed for marketing, game development, and character consistency in AI video production. Whether I'm fine-tuning a 2B parameter model like Sarvam-1 or building WebGL games in Unity, my goal is to ensure the gap between idea and output is closed by a reliable, repeatable system. Representing India at the ASEAN-China-India Youth Leadership Summit in 2024 was a defining moment for me, fueling my passion for global collaboration. Beyond engineering, I’m usually busy with pixel art, basketball, gaming, or travelling.
